IDEAS:

- Simple game loop: A dude with a sword that can either attack close range or throw it;
- Every kill grants a boost or combo of some sort;
- Beat the level, buy a boost of some sort, next level, repeat.

MECHANICS (THOUGHTS):

- Some obstacles randomly placed each level and with enemies increasingly harder;
- After each kill, grant some combo or boost;
- After each level, the player can choose some reward.

MECHANICS IMPLEMENTED:

- Simple Grid with automatic creation, editable in height, length and tile size (to be refined later on);
- Tile Detection and Communication with the board through signals (tiles are buttons);
- Player Movement on the board and clear conditions for movement;
